About Me

Actually, I am working as teaching fellow in statistics at the Paul Valéry University in Montpellier in France.

Previously, I was a PhD student in statistics at the Institut Montpellierain Alexander Grothendieck (IMAG) in the University of Montpellier in France. This PhD was jointly supervised by Catherine Trottier and Xavier Bry.

My publications can be found in Google Scholar, HAL, ORCID, ResearchGate and on my web page.

You can find here my Curriculum Vitae in French or in English.
